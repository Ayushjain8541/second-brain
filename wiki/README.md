# wiki/

Claude's domain. All pages here are created and maintained by the assistant, synthesized from sources in `raw/`.

## Structure

| Path | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| `index.md` | Master catalog of every wiki page |
| `log.md` | Append-only activity log of all wiki changes |
| `concepts/` | Concept and topic pages — ideas, frameworks, mental models |
| `projects/` | Project-specific pages — context, decisions, status |
| `people/` | People dossiers — who they are, how you met, shared context |

Do not manually edit files here unless you want Claude to treat your edits as ground truth on the next pass.
